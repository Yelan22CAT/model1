# Model 1 v1.0 — Minimal Public Core

> Copy-safe public core.  
> First-person stop-loss guardrail only.  
> Not therapy, diagnosis, persuasion, profiling, automation, or execution authority.

---

## 0. Scope Lock

Use this only for a first-person question:

```text
I am considering a next consequential step.
Do I need to proceed, slow down, freeze, or exit?
```

Reject use if the goal is to:

- analyze another person's motives or value,
- label, rank, diagnose, or profile someone,
- persuade, pressure, manipulate, or optimize influence,
- automate action from the signal,
- replace legal, medical, safety, or professional judgment.

---

## 1. Input

```text
Situation:
Next consequential step I am considering:
Boundary I do not want to surrender:
Cost already rising:
What becomes harder to reverse if I continue:
```

If any required field is unknown, mark it as `unknown` rather than filling it with assumption.

---

## 2. Check

Ask four checks in order:

```text
1. Boundary:
Is a stated boundary being crossed, softened, or removed just to keep the process moving?

2. Cost:
Is time, energy, exposure, responsibility, money, safety, or integrity being consumed faster than verified value is appearing?

3. Reversibility:
If I continue, will it become harder to stop, recover, withdraw, or return to baseline?

4. Judgment:
Am I being pushed to surrender final judgment to urgency, emotion, another person, a system, or AI output?
```

---

## 3. Signal

Return only one signal:

```text
Green:
No stop-loss trigger is identified from the stated first-person input.
Continue observing. No guarantee is given.

Yellow:
Cost, ambiguity, or reduced reversibility is increasing.
Slow down, verify evidence, preserve options, and avoid irreversible movement.

Red:
A hard boundary or irreversible-cost risk is present.
Freeze or exit is reasonable. The human user decides.
```

---

## 4. Human Final Gate

No signal authorizes action.

```text
The signal is not a command.
The signal is not proof.
The signal is not permission.
The signal does not execute.
The final decision remains with the human user.
```

---

## 5. Minimal Output Format

```text
Signal: Green / Yellow / Red
Reason in one sentence:
Boundary status:
Cost / reversibility status:
Recommended next posture: proceed / slow / freeze / exit
Human Final Gate: decision remains with me
```

---

## Hard Rule

```text
Keep what helps me identify whether I should stop.
Remove what helps me influence, push, rank, or control others.
```

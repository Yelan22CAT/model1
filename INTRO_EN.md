# Model 1 v1.0 Intro

> Update date: 2026-06-06

## Purpose

A minimal first-person guardrail before action.

## Check

1. Real-world consequence?
2. Source verified?
3. Safe stop or rollback?

## Signals

- Green: proceed.
- Yellow: verify first.
- Red: freeze.

## Boundary

No prediction, manipulation, or final decision outsourcing.

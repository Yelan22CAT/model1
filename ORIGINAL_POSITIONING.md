# ORIGINAL POSITIONING & SCOPE STATEMENT  
# 原创定位与使用边界声明

## 1. Nature of this work | 本项目的性质

**EN**  
Model 1 v1.0 is an independent, experience-driven public protocol developed outside an academic institution. Its public form is a **minimal stop-loss guardrail protocol**: a narrow safety brake before a consequential next action.

It is not presented as an AI model, a predictive model, or an execution system.

**中文**  
Model 1 v1.0 是一项独立形成、源于现实经验的公开协议。它的公开形态是**最小止损护栏协议**：在人执行下一步高后果动作之前，提供一个窄范围的安全刹车。

它不是 AI 模型，不是预测模型，也不是执行系统。

## 2. Scope | 适用范围

**EN**  
The public protocol accepts first-person questions about the user's own boundary, cost exposure, reversibility, and proposed next step.

“Pre-execution” means before the next consequential step is taken, including within a situation already underway. It does not authorize speculative forecasting about another person, relationship, organization, or market.

**中文**  
公开协议只接收第一人称问题：使用者自己的边界、成本暴露、可逆性与下一步动作。

“执行前”指下一步高后果动作发生之前，包括已经处于某个现实场景中的下一步；它不授权对他人、关系、组织或市场进行投机式预测。

## 3. What this work is not | 本项目不属于什么

**EN**  
This work is not:

- therapy, counseling, emotional support, or medical/legal advice,
- a diagnostic or profiling tool for other people,
- a coercion, persuasion, manipulation, or optimization toolkit,
- an automated decision engine or agent execution framework,
- a universal method for life or social judgment.

**中文**  
本项目不是：

- 心理咨询、情绪支持、医疗或法律建议；
- 对他人进行诊断、画像或贴标签的工具；
- 影响、操控、拿捏或说服优化的工具；
- 自动裁决引擎或 Agent 执行框架；
- 可泛化为所有生活或社会问题的万能方法。

## 4. Core intent | 核心目的

**EN**  
The protocol exists to detect whether a person should pause before continuing along a path whose cost is rising and whose reversibility is decreasing.

It may output a bounded signal. It does not decide, act, authorize, or assume responsibility.

**中文**  
本协议的目的，是在一条路径的成本持续上升、可逆性持续下降时，帮助使用者识别自己是否应在继续之前暂停。

它可以输出有限信号，但不替人裁决、不替人行动、不授权动作，也不承担责任。

## 5. Human Final Gate | 人类最终裁决

**EN**  
Every consequential decision remains with the user. AI output is candidate signal only and must not be treated as fact, permission, or final authority.

**中文**  
所有高后果决策最终归使用者本人。AI 输出只可作为候选信号，不得视为事实、许可或最终权威。

## 6. AI assistance disclosure | AI 辅助披露

**EN**  
AI may assist with drafting, formatting, translation, compression, or checking whether already stated boundaries were preserved during decoding. AI may not define the project's scope, judgment rules, safety boundaries, or publication decision.

**中文**  
AI 可协助起草、排版、翻译、压缩，或检查既定边界在解码过程中是否被保留；但不得替作者定义项目范围、判断规则、安全边界或发布决定。
